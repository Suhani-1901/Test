About

Purpose of the trivia quiz

They Can Help Increase Your Knowledge Base One of the best things about playing trivia is that it can help increase your knowledge base. This is because you're constantly learning new facts and information while you're playing. Not only will this make you smarter, but they can also serve as great conversation starters!

How users can participate

These fun, communal quiz games are open for anyone who wishes to participate, and are totally free to join. Getting in on the action is as simple as grabbing a few friends, devising a team name, and putting your heads together to answer questions centered around a variety of different subjects, topics, and formats.
